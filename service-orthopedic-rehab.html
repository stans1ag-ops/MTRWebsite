Orthopedic Rehab

Orthopedic rehab helps people recover from injuries or pain in the muscles, joints, bones, ligaments, or tendons. This may include problems with the back, neck, shoulder, hip, knee, ankle, or other areas of the body. Your therapist works with you to reduce pain, improve movement, rebuild strength, and help you return to everyday activities with more comfort and confidence.
