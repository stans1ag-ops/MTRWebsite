About Mountain Town Rehab

A family-owned clinic rooted in the Mt Pleasant community, built on trust, compassion, and a passion for helping you heal.

Our Story

Mountain Town Rehab was founded with a simple but powerful belief: physical therapy should be personal. As a family-owned and operated clinic, we've been proudly serving the Mt Pleasant, Michigan community since 2001 — building lasting relationships with our patients and neighbors along the way.

From the beginning, our approach has been different. We don't shuffle patients through a revolving door — we provide dedicated, one-on-one care tailored to your unique needs and goals. Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or getting back in the game after a sports injury, you'll always work directly with a licensed provider who knows your story.

Our clinic is more than a workplace; it's a family. Mark and Sue Stansberry built Mountain Town Rehab on the values of integrity, compassion, and excellence — values that guide every treatment plan we create and every interaction we have.
