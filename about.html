About Mountain Town Rehab

A family-owned clinic rooted in the Mt Pleasant community, built on trust, compassion, and a passion for helping you heal.

Our Story

Mountain Town Rehab was founded with a simple but powerful belief: physical therapy should be personal. As a family-owned and operated clinic, we confidently treat each patient as we would want our family members to be treated. We've proudly served the central Michigan area since 2001 — building lasting relationships with our patients and neighbors along the way.

From the beginning, our approach has been different, it’s personal. We provide dedicated, one-on-one care tailored to your unique needs and goals. Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or getting back in the game after a sports injury, you'll always work directly with a licensed provider who knows your story and understands your goals. We feel blessed that you have chosen our team to be part of your recovery and wellness plan.

Our clinic is more than a workplace; it's a family. Mark and Sue Stansberry built Mountain Town Rehab on the values of integrity, compassion, and excellence — values that guide every treatment plan we create and every interaction we have.
