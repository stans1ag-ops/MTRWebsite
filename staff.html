Mark Stansberry

Owner / PT, DPT, FAAOMPT

Mark is originally from Mt. Pleasant and is a graduate of Sacred Heart Academy. He earned his Bachelor's degree in Health Science from the University of Michigan-Flint and completed his Master's in Physical Therapy at U of M-Flint in 1992. He earned his doctorate in physical therapy from Rocky Mountain University in Provo, Utah. He is a graduate of the URSA Foundation under the direction of Dr. Loren Rex. He has extensive training in manual techniques and has completed numerous courses on Muscle Energy Techniques (MET) including coursework instructed by Dr. Fred Mitchell and Dr. Jay Sandweiss. Mark is certified in Intramuscular Manual Therapy through the Travell Series. He is a member of the American Physical Therapy Association (Orthopedic and Private Practice Sections), and the Michigan Physical Therapy Association. In 2015, Mark obtained his Orthopedic Clinical Specialist certification. He received his fellowship training at the Institute of Therapeutic Science and is currently an adjunct faculty and lectures on a variety of topics including knee injuries and blood flow restriction. He is currently adjunct faculty at Central Michigan University in the Physical Therapy graduate program and serves on the admittance panel for the school. Prior to opening his own business, he was the Director of Rehabilitation Services for Central Michigan Community Hospital.

Mark opened Mountain Town Rehab in 2001. He treats primarily orthopedic injuries, but enjoys treating an eclectic population with a variety of interventions. He is active in the community and enjoys the company of his family; wife, Sue, son and daughter-in-law, Alex and Theresa, and son Kyle.

Mark's patient-centered approach to providing care has created a dedicated clientele and developed a strong referral base. In fact, Mountain Town Rehab has received referrals from over 1800 physicians throughout Michigan and from a variety of states. Mark has selected highly qualified individuals to form his Mountain Town Rehab team. He has treated athletes at the youth, high school, collegiate, professional and Olympic levels. Mountain Town Rehab is the premiere choice for physical therapy in Central Michigan and your choice for personal, quality care.

Alex Stansberry

DPT

Alex is from Mount Pleasant and graduated high school from Sacred Heart Academy in Mt. Pleasant. He earned his undergraduate degree, a B.S. in Health Fitness, from CMU in 2010. He continued his CMU education and graduated in May 2013 with a Doctorate in Physical Therapy. He received his Orthopedic Clinical Specialist certification in 2016. Alex came to Mountain Town Rehab in May 2013.

Alex has clinical experience in outpatient settings with patients of various orthopedic and neurologic conditions, as well as experience in inpatient rehabilitation with patients of various neurologic conditions. Alex is interested in working with patients who have a broad range of orthopedic conditions, with more specific interests in working with individuals who have sports related injuries. He is certified in Intramuscular Manual Therapy (IMT) and Blood Flow Restriction Training (BFRT) and has extensive training in Graston technique.

In his personal time, Alex enjoys spending time with family and friends. He has two boys and participates in triathlons. He also enjoys biking, golfing, playing volleyball, reading, and camping.
