Join Our Family

Explore rewarding clinical careers at Mountain Town Rehab. We're looking for passionate PTs & PTAs who share our commitment to exceptional, one-on-one patient care.

Why Build Your Career With Us?

We believe that happy, supported therapists deliver the best patient care. Here is what makes our clinic a unique place to grow professionally.

1-on-1 Patient Care

We reject the high-volume, double-booked clinic model. Here, you get the dedicated, individual time with patients necessary to guide them to true recovery.

Family-Owned Warmth

Owned and operated by practicing therapists. We understand clinical demands and treat our staff with flexibility, respect, and support.

Mentorship & CEUs

We actively support continuing education to elevate your practice. New graduates benefit from structural, experienced clinical mentoring.

Central MI Community

Mt. Pleasant offers a friendly university-town atmosphere with great schools, low cost of living, and proximity to beautiful Michigan lakes.

Clinical

Physical Therapist (PT)

Full-Time or Part-Time | Mt. Pleasant, MI

Apply For This Role

Key Responsibilities:

  • Perform comprehensive initial evaluations and formulate customized plans of care.
  • Deliver compassionate, evidence-based, 1-on-1 therapeutic interventions.
  • Collaborate with other clinical staff, referring physicians, and patient families.
  • Maintain clean, compliant documentation aligned with professional standards.

Requirements & Benefits:

Candidates must hold an active Physical Therapist License in the State of Michigan (or be eligible to receive it). We offer competitive compensation, comprehensive health benefits, generous paid time off (PTO), a continuing education allowance, and 401(k) matching.

Clinical Support

Physical Therapist Assistant (PTA)

Full-Time or Part-Time | Mt. Pleasant, MI

Apply For This Role

Key Responsibilities:

  • Administer physical therapy treatments under the direction and supervision of a licensed PT.
  • Deliver premium 1-on-1 care, assisting patients with therapeutic exercises and functional training.
  • Track and document patient progress daily to collaborate closely with the supervising PT.
  • Maintain a safe, welcoming, and sterile clinical environment.

Requirements & Benefits:

Candidates must hold an active Physical Therapist Assistant License in the State of Michigan (or be eligible to receive it). We offer competitive pay, healthcare coverage options, paid vacation, continuing education support, and retirement matching.
